Evidence & Verification

  • Responsible parties: CISO/CRO (NCA, SAMA), DPO/Privacy Office (PDPL).
  • Verification methods: policy review, configuration checks, log sampling, test results, training records.
  • Timelines: incident (24h NCA; 72h PDPL), recurring audits (annual; BCP twice yearly), SAMA monthly reports.
